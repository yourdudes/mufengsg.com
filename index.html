What mufeng does

Small, hands-on advisory for work that needs to become clearer.

mufeng works with founders, operators, and educators who need an outside view and a practical way forward. The work is simple: listen carefully, make the situation visible, and build tools the team can keep using.

Services

A useful outside hand when the next step is not obvious.

Work can be a short conversation, a workshop, or a few weeks of hands-on support. The shape depends on the problem, not on a fixed consulting package.

Method

A plain way of working.

Listen

Start with the real situation.

What is happening now, who is involved, and what would make the work easier?

Map

Put the moving parts on one page.

Goals, people, decisions, risks, and constraints become visible enough to work with.

Make

Build something the team can use.

A session plan, brief, roadmap, learning flow, or operating routine, depending on the need.

Hand over

Leave the work in usable shape.

The output should be clear enough for someone else to pick up without a long explanation.
